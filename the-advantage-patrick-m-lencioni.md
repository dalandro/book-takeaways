## Misc points, in no particular order
- The importance of alignment
- The idea of different levels of cultural values - like aspirational vs inherent  
- The idea of overcommunicating key ideas. People often don't take a message seriously unless they see it's importance demonstrated. This can be through repeated communication of the message. This might seem like you're risking seeming like a broken record, but you're actually showing that it was never just some offhand whim, but something that you are serious about and committed to it. This helps reinforce alignment.
- The equal weighting of importance between organizational health and intelligence/skills
- Clarity - 4 disciplines:
  - Build cohesion - e.g. trust, conflict handling, committment, accountability, results focus
  - Create clarity - on purpose, on behaviours, on goals, on what is most important right now, and who does what
  - Overcommunicate clarity
    - Communication can go in multiple directions - cascading, top-down, uptward, lateral. Take advantage of all of them, depending on the situation. 
  - Reinforce clarity
