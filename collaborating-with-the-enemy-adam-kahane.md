# Collaborating With The Enemy: How to Work with People You Don't Agree with or Like or Trust
**Author:**  Adam Kahane

---

## Misc points, in no particular order

- From memory, the book constructed a model of different approaches to collaboration.
- It seems to accept that yes sometimes you should even take an authoritative approach, given certain conditions.
- I remember it was a very flexible set of approaches. They don't prescribe an exact solution ahead of time, but instead requires exploring the conflict progressively to find out more information and come to realisations.
